OM Yoga

A location-based and responsive web app.

The problem:

There is not a strong space in the wellness industry-catering to those solely interested in yoga-where one can look up yoga classes nearby.

The solution:

This is where OM Yoga came to fruition! It's an app that suggests popular classes nearby, allows users to search for classes, look at reviews, and purchase classes-all while using distinct filter capabilities!

User Personas

I interviewed individuals between the ages of 26-55 who were full time, part time and retired. I got to learn about why they practice yoga and/or why they're interested in starting, what they look for in a yoga class and their frustrations when it comes to signing up for an online class. I discovered cruical pain points such as driving and tedious cancellations and found that looking at reviews, leaving reviews, and using filters were essential for those looking to use a yoga app.

User flow diagram

Once I discovered my pain points, I began to think about the interactions and flows within OM Yoga. Based on the pain points and desires of potential users, I created a user flow diagram. Have a look!

Mid fidelity wireframes

After implementing all of the feedback I received from my prototypes made in Figma, I put together the mid-fidelity wireframes, implementing some of the copy, but not all of it. I made the content boxes even with the use of grids so that way when it came to high fidelity wireframes, I could fill in the context with more copy, photography, and colors!

Moodboard

I wanted the feel for OM Yoga to be soft and inviting, creating a sense of community (which was also a noted desire pulled from my user interviews). To achieve this I stuck with soft colors and decided I wanted a bold blue for the accent, which you'll see very subtly throughout the app.

Mockups

After doing my second round of usability testing, I put together my responsive web design mockups, making sure I included aspects to target "tedious cancellations" (the booked classes screen), "driving and traffic" (the live stream option) and advanced filter systems.
